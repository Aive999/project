We are a diversified, regional law firm oriented towards trial practice. We resolve disputes ethically, thoroughly and cost-effectively. We also provide business planning advice with an eye towards avoiding disputes and speeding their resolution.

Through our wide-area computer network, our three-to-six lawyer offices can share and accumulate large-firm style resources, while maintaining the host of benefits that come from small-firm practice.

Technology, accountability, experience and enthusiastic effort are the ingredients we combine to assure our clients' advantage.

Our work frequently involves all varieties of insurance issues -- from insurance agents' errors and omissions defense, coverage, regulatory matters, to reinsurance disputes.

Another core part of our work centers around all forms of transportation risks: by road, railroad, boat or air.

Our third largest practice area is energy-related liability and contractual claims.

For further details on our practice and experience, please review any of the list below:

THESE ARE OUR AREAS OF PRACTICE:

ADMIRALTY

Cargo, collision, sinkings, strandings, Jones Act, Maintenance and Cure, MEL, passenger liabilities, limitation of liability, hull claims, terminal operators', wharfingers', marina operators', and ship repair claims.

AUTO, COMMERCIAL AUTO, AND TRUCK LIABILITY

Defense of liability claims, and advice on coverage of liability incidents arising out of personal auto, commercial auto including buses, delivery services, ambulance, and rental cars. Trucking claims are handled by attorneys with years of truck experience and familiarity with the equipment, and with regulatory and reporting requirements.

AVIATION

Passenger and bystander personal injury claims, products and component manufacturers liability defense, airport and FBO claims, FAA regulatory and enforcement action defense.

COMPREHENSIVE GENERAL LIABILITY

Premises liability and advertising injury defense, hotel, motel and assisted living center liabilities, sporting and recreational activities liabilities, construction and maintenance liabilities.

CARGO

Prosecution of damaged and lost cargo claims, and defense of cargo claims under bills of lading or statutory limitations.

CLASS ACTIONS

Leading, liaison, administrative, or specialty counsel assignments in connection with class actions in federal and state courts.

CONSTRUCTION

Contractor's liabilities, including completed operations, workers' comp disputes, structural and engineering-related claims.

EMPLOYMENT

Discrimination, harassment and retaliatory discharge claims.

ENVIRONMENTAL AND TOXIC TORTS

Sudden, accidental, and long-term exposure liability defense and coverage disputes, including management of punitive damages exposures where present.

ERRORS AND OMISSIONS

Professional liability defense for: attorneys, accountants, directors and officers, architects and engineers, physicians, veterinarians, and other health care professionals.

EXCESS/UMBRELLA

Monitoring and quality control for significant exposure claims.

F.E.L.A. AND L.H.W.C.A

Rail, harbor workers, and other statutory employees' liability defense.

HEALTH CARE INSURANCE

ERISA, COBRA, and related health care insurance issues.

INSURANCE COVERAGE AND CONSTRUCTION

Coverage is a significant part of the firm's work. We draft wordings and offer advice under state laws, on the effect of existing wordings. We resolve coverage disputes through direct negotiation, mediation, arbitration, or litigation. We offer advice and supervision of claims adjustment practices and procedures.

INSURANCE INSOLVENCY AND REGULATION

Our locations in and near state capitols allow us convenient access to the insurance commissioners with whom we maintain collegial and respectful relations. We act on behalf of insurers, brokers, and agents in regulatory compliance and in defense of administrative actions.
